Breakfast Quesadilla

Moreover, you can serve such a royal breakfast within 10 minutes. In addition to breakfast, I also like to make these quesadillas for dinner - try it if you don't have much time to prepare breakfast in the morning. You can change up this breakfast quesadilla recipe every time and use whatever ingredients you have on hand. Ham, tuna, various types of vegetables, cheese or legumes also serve as a filling. This quesadilla is rich in proteins and healthy fats, calorically, one full portion is around 500 kcal.

10min
Servings
1
Ingredients
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • a bit of salt and black pepper
  • a handful of baby spinach
  • 50g of grated mozzarella light
  • 1 tortilla
  • 50 grams of cherry tomatoes
Instructions
  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan.
  2. Break the eggs and mix them in a bowl with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan (like an omelet).
  4. Reduce the heat of the burner to low, place the washed baby spinach on top of the eggs and sprinkle them with half of the cheese. Wait about 30 seconds for the cheese to melt.
  5. Place a tortilla on top of this mixture and press it hard with a spatula to the bottom of the pan so that the individual ingredients are connected.
  6. Then, using a spatula, turn the entire mixture upside down, so the tortilla will be on the bottom and the eggs on top.
  7. Then increase the temperature slightly and fry the tortilla for another 2 minutes until golden.
  8. Sprinkle the rest of the cheese on top of the eggs and fold the quesadilla with the filling in half.
  9. Press down the closed quesadilla with a spatula so that the individual ingredients are combined as much as possible and fry it for another minute.
  10. Finally, cut the quesadilla into smaller triangles and serve them warm with cherry tomatoes.

Broccoli salad

This salad with broccoli is a great source of protein, fiber and many trace elements, and on the other hand, you will find relatively few fat and calories in it. You can serve it on its own, or serve it with fried tofu or chicken.

20 minutes
Servings
3
Ingredients
  • 500 g of broccoli
  • 250 g of cottage cheese
  • 100 g of dried quinoa
  • 80 g of corn
  • 1 soup spoon of oil
  • 1 spring onion
  • 2 soup spoon of chopped parsley or chives
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • a bit of kari curry
  • a bit of garlic
Instructions
  1. Boil the quinoa in twice the volume of water until soft.
  2. Wash the broccoli and chop it finely.
  3. In a bowl, mix cottage cheese with corn, chopped herbs and spring onion, olive oil, spices and salt.
  4. We also add chopped, raw broccoli and cooked quinoa and mix the salad thoroughly.
  5. At the end, we can season the salad with pepper or salt. We serve it either immediately or after it has been stored in the refrigerator.

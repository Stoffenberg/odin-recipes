Fit Egg Wrap

Try this perfect recipe for a fit egg wrap filled with spinach and cheese. It is low in carbohydrates, rich in protein, easy to prepare and will definitely make your taste buds dance! Try it as a quick dinner, weekend breakfast or snack for work. You will have it ready within 15 minutes.

In this breakfast recipe, we will prepare the eggs in the style of an omelet, which we will use as a wrap. Spread it with cream cheese and then fill it with spinach and cheese. We heat them together for a while and finally we just roll up the wrap and we can enjoy a great meal full of nutrients.

How can you customize this recipe? Add, for example, feta cheese, dried tomatoes, chopped olives, fresh herbs, arugula, steamed mushrooms, smoked salmon slices or grated and steamed zucchini or pumpkin to the filling. The possibilities are endless and it depends only on you which ingredients you choose. This combination with spinach and mozzarella is definitely my favorite - it contains ingredients that I almost always have at home and their combination never disappoints.

15 minutes
Servings
1
Ingredients
  • 2 big eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic spice
  • 1 teaspoon of butter or oil
  • a bit of chilly
  • 2 big spoons of cream cheese
  • 30g of grated mozarella
  • a handful of baby spinach
  • salt and black pepper
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, use a whisk to mix the broken eggs with chili, garlic pepper, salt and ground black pepper (to taste).
  2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan, spreading it to the sides of the pan as if we were making a pancake.
  4. Cover the pan for 2 minutes, until the egg pancake is golden on the bottom and cooked on top.
  5. Then spread the eggs with cream cheese on top, sprinkle with washed spinach and grated cheese and leave everything on the hot and covered pan for another minute so that the spinach softens and the cheese melts.
  6. Finally, roll the wrap tightly, cut it in half and serve it warm.
